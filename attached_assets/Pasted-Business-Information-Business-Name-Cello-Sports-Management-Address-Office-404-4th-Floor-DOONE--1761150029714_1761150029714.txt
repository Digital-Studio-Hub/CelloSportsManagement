Project Goal
Build a clean, responsive, and visually appealing website that reflects the energy and professionalism of Cello Sports Management.
The website should inspire confidence in athletes, partners, and sponsors while providing clear information about services, programs, and contact options.

Core Requirements
1. Website Type
A corporate + portfolio-style website for a sports management agency.
It should highlight athlete representation, training programs, events, and sponsorship opportunities.

2. Branding & Design
Use a modern, athletic-inspired design — strong typography, bold imagery, and energetic color tones (e.g., black, white, silver, and electric blue/green).

Include subtle micro animations (scroll reveals, hover effects, and fade transitions).

Ensure consistent branding with rounded corners, soft shadows, and clean layout grids.

The tone should balance professionalism and youthful energy.

Use sports-related photography and motion visuals (athletes in action, teamwork, stadium shots).

3. Website Structure & Sections
Hero Section
Full-width hero banner with a bold headline:
“Empowering Athletes. Building Futures.”

Subtext: “Professional sports management, representation, and performance development.”

Include a dynamic sports video or background image.

Prominent Call-to-Action (CTA): “Get In Touch” or “Represent Me”.

About Us
Overview of Cello Sports Management — who they are, their mission, and what sets them apart.

Mention leadership team (Siphelele and Seymour) and their expertise in athlete management and sports coordination.

Include high-quality team photos and short bios.

Services
Showcase main service areas with icons or cards:

Athlete Representation

Sports Marketing & Sponsorships

Event Management

Talent Scouting & Recruitment

Training & Development Programs
Each card should include a short description and a “Learn More” link.

Athletes / Portfolio
Display featured athletes under management with profile cards (image, name, sport, team, short description).

Include a lightbox gallery for highlight reels or images.

Option to filter by sport category.

Events & Highlights
Showcase recent or upcoming events managed or sponsored by Cello Sports.

Include event posters, photo galleries, and recap videos.

Option for visitors to register or inquire about partnerships.

Testimonials
Include quotes from athletes, partners, and organizations.

Optionally use a carousel layout for client feedback.

Gallery
A visual showcase of events, athletes, and activities.

Enable lightbox or modal previews with smooth transitions.

Contact Section
Contact form with fields: Name, Email, Phone, Message.

Include a Google Map embed for office location.
